Introduction



Experience the soul of Kathmandu in every sip at our local tea shop, where each cup is a journey through the flavors of Nepal. From the lush hills to your hands, we bring you the finest blends of tea, infused with the warmth and hospitality of our culture. Come, unwind and savor a moment of tranquility amidst the hustle and bustle of the city. Let our teas take you on a voyage of taste and tradition, and leave you feeling rejuvenated, one sip at a time.

About us

At our tea shop, we believe that tea is not just a beverage, but a way of life. With a passion for quality and authenticity, we bring you the best teas from the hills of Nepal and beyond, handpicked and blended to perfection. Our journey began with a simple idea: to create a space where people could unwind, connect and savor the joys of tea. From that vision, we have grown into a community of tea lovers, where every cup is an invitation to explore new flavors and cultures. We are committed to sustainability, fair trade and supporting local farmers, ensuring that each sip you take makes a positive impact. Whether you are a connoisseur or a casual drinker, our tea shop welcomes you to join us in celebrating the art and science of tea.

Our work

At our tea shop, our work goes beyond just brewing the perfect cup of tea. We strive to make a positive impact on the communities we serve and the environment we live in. We source our teas from local farmers, ensuring fair trade practices and supporting sustainable agriculture. Our packaging is eco-friendly, reducing waste and minimizing our carbon footprint. We also collaborate with local artists and musicians, creating a space where creativity and culture thrive. Through our work, we hope to inspire a sense of social responsibility and promote a holistic approach to business. We believe that tea is not just a product, but a catalyst for change, and we are committed to making a difference, one cup at a time.
